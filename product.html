product

I was inspired by the coastal experience of our community. Big hop's taste, which stands out like a cliff at low tide in the Bay of Fundy evokes memories of South Shore's soft white sand and meets the crunchy lager finish. It is the characteristic of leopard tropical fruits and the expressive power of lager drinks. It is a hybrid warm-up fermentation lager with IPA sensitivity and has a strong bitter taste with a medium but strong yet clean finish. It's bold, refreshing, and blurry. It's the same as our Nova Scotia coastline of 7579 kilometers.
